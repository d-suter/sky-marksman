Game Rules - Sky Marksman
Welcome to Sky Marksman! In "Sky Marksman", your aim is to shoot ducks that fly across your screen. Hereâ€™s what you need to know:

Starting the Game: Click the 'Start Game' button to begin. The game starts with 3 ducks.

Gameplay: Shoot ducks by clicking on them with your mouse.

Levels: The game continuously progresses in levels. Every 5 seconds, if you haven't lost, 2 more ducks will be added to the screen.

Losing the Game: If any duck reaches the bottom of the screen, the game ends. A 'Game Over' message will display the level you reached.

Winning the Game: There is no final level; you play to achieve the highest level and score possible. Challenge yourself to see how far you can go!

Stay sharp, and happy hunting!
